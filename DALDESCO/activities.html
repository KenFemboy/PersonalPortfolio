CLIMBS' 51st GA

April 27, 2023

The 51st General Assembly (GA) of CLIMBS, or the Cooperative and Life Insurance Management and Business Support Service, is an annual gathering of members, stakeholders, and leaders within the cooperative sector. The GA serves as a platform for CLIMBS to report on its achievements, discuss important issues, and make decisions regarding the organization's direction and future plans. During the 51st GA, participants can expect a range of activities, including keynote speeches, presentations, panel discussions, and workshops. These sessions may cover a variety of topics relevant to the cooperative and insurance sectors, such as financial performance, strategic initiatives, regulatory updates, industry trends, and social impact.

After The Cooperative Month Celebration Parade

October 5, 2019

The Cooperative Month Celebration Parade aims to raise awareness about the cooperative sector's contributions to the local economy, sustainable development, and poverty alleviation. It serves as a platform to showcase the diversity of cooperatives, including agricultural, consumer, housing, credit, and other types of cooperatives. During the parade, cooperatives may creatively depict their products, services, and activities through floats, themed vehicles, or displays. These visuals may highlight the cooperative's impact on agriculture, livelihoods, education, health, and other sectors. Musical bands, cultural performances, and other forms of entertainment often accompany the procession, adding to the festive atmosphere.

Social Responsibility - Brigada Eskwela

May 30, 2018

Brigada Eskwela promotes a sense of ownership and pride in the community's educational institutions. It encourages active participation and shared responsibility among various stakeholders, fostering a spirit of cooperation and unity. By engaging in these activities, volunteers not only contribute to the physical improvement of the school but also create a positive and welcoming atmosphere for students, enhancing their overall learning experience. The event also serves as an opportunity for schools to raise awareness about their needs and challenges, allowing community members and organizations to provide support, donations, or resources as needed. It helps foster a stronger connection between schools and the community, promoting a culture of education and collective responsibility. Brigada Eskwela is not only about physical improvements; it also highlights the importance of education and the role of schools in shaping the future of the nation. It brings attention to the significance of investing in quality education, promoting inclusive learning environments, and empowering students to reach their full potential.
